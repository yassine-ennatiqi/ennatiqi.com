payment methods

Cash on delivery (COD)

Cash on delivery is one of the payment methods available on our store. Payment on delivery means that the shopper can shop through our online store and choose the product he desires, then place the order and choose the payment method upon receipt, which means that the payment process is postponed until The customer receives the product he ordered electronically. We will send the product to the agreed upon location (city, neighborhood, home, or another place), and then payment will be made.

Payment via bank transfer

Payment via bank transfer is one of the payment methods available on our store. Payment is made by transferring the agreed upon amount to our bank account, or via a bank transfer, or via one of the money transfer agencies in the agreed upon name, after which the product is sent to the agreed upon location.

Payment via PayPal

Payment via PayPal transfer is one of the methods available on our store. Payment is made by transferring the agreed upon amount to our PayPal account, after which the product is sent to the agreed upon location.
